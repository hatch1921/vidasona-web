Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

The short version: Vidasona stores all your health data on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal health information. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

Vidasona does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no data collection infrastructure.

The information you log in Vidasona — including symptoms, moods, journal entries, and health data — is stored exclusively in encrypted local storage on your iPhone. This data is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

2. iCloud Backup

Vidasona offers an optional iCloud backup feature. If you enable this feature, your encrypted health data is backed up to your personal iCloud Drive account. This backup is stored in your own iCloud storage and is governed by Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy. HatchApps LLC has no access to your iCloud data.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Vidasona does not collect or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your health or medical data
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers
  • Your usage patterns or analytics data
  • Any information about how you use the app

4. Third-Party Services

Vidasona does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not use tracking pixels, cookies, or any other tracking technology.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone because we do not have access to your data. When you generate a health report and choose to share it, that sharing is performed entirely by your device using iOS standard share functionality. You choose where the report goes. We are never involved in that process.

6. Data Security

Your health data is stored in your device's encrypted local storage. Your PIN is stored in iOS Keychain, which uses hardware-level encryption. We have designed Vidasona so that a security breach at HatchApps LLC would have zero impact on your health data, because we do not hold any of it.

7. Children's Privacy

Vidasona is rated 17+ and is intended for adult women. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 17.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version at vidasona.app/privacy and update the date above. Because we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

HatchApps LLC
support@vidasona.app

10. Medical Disclaimer

Vidasona is a personal health journal and is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions. The reports generated by Vidasona are personal health summaries based on information you entered and are not medical records.
